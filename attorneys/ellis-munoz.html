About Ellis

Ellis Munoz's connection to the legal profession runs deep. Growing up in a legal family, he worked alongside his father, an attorney in Conroe, Texas, during his high school and college years. This early exposure instilled in him a passion for helping clients navigate difficult times and fighting for justice on their behalf.

Before pursuing his legal career, Ellis earned a degree in mechanical engineering, developing the technical and analytical skills that now help him understand complex accident reconstructions, medical evidence, and the intricate details that can make or break a personal injury case.

He went on to graduate Cum Laude from South Texas College of Law Houston, demonstrating his commitment to legal excellence and advocacy. Following his admission to the Texas Bar, Ellis joined a personal injury firm as a litigating attorney, where he managed his own docket while refining his skills in client advocacy and case strategy.

Today, Ellis serves as an attorney at Nguyen & Associates Injury Law Firm, where he continues to advocate zealously for injury victims. His engineering background gives him a distinct advantage when analyzing accident scenes, understanding mechanical failures, and building compelling cases for his clients.

Outside of the office, Ellis enjoys spending time with his family and his Boston Terrier, Barnaby.
