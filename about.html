Home/About

A Houston firm, built for Houston people.

Nguyen & Associates is a personal injury law firm founded on one idea: the lawyer should always reach the client, and the client should always reach the lawyer.

Why this firm exists

Most injury firms in Houston operate the same way: one lawyer handles intake, another handles the file, a paralegal runs day-to-day, and the client rarely talks to the attorney whose name is on the door.

We were built to work differently. The attorney who answers the first call is the attorney on the case — through medical treatment, through negotiation, through trial if it goes that far. When you call, we pick up. When you need an answer, we give one.

What we handle — and what we don't

We practice only personal injury and wrongful death. That's the whole firm. No side business in divorce, estate planning, or business law. The focus is deliberate: personal injury is a specialized trade, and anyone who tries to do it alongside five other practice areas gets outworked by a firm that does nothing else.

Language access that respects you

Houston is one of the most diverse cities in America. More than 145 languages are spoken in Harris County, and many of our neighbors don't conduct their personal lives in English.

Most personal injury firms handle this through translator services billed by the minute, or by handing key conversations to staff who weren't there for the legal strategy. By the time something gets translated back to the client, important details have slipped.

We do it differently. Our intake and case-management team speaks Spanish and Vietnamese. From the moment you call, the people walking you through your case — explaining what's happening, translating the important conversations with your attorney, making sure paperwork is fully understood before you sign — speak the language you do.

When you have a question at 9pm, leave a voicemail in Spanish or Vietnamese. Someone who speaks your language will return the call. When the insurance company sends paperwork, we explain what it means. When you come into the office to meet with your attorney, you don't sit alone trying to follow what's being said.

It's not "se habla español" on a sticker. It's making sure the legal system — which was built for English speakers — doesn't fail you because of how it was built.

How we charge

Contingency fee on every personal-injury case. No fee unless we win. No retainer, no hourly rate, no hidden costs. Our fee is a pre-agreed percentage of what we recover — and if we don't recover anything, we don't charge.
