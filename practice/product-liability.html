Houston defective product lawyer.

When a product injures you, Texas law provides powerful strict-liability remedies against manufacturers, distributors, and retailers. We've handled defective auto parts, power tools, medical devices, and more.

Free case review (713) 842-9442

Quick answer: Under Texas Civil Practice & Remedies Code Chapter 82 and common-law strict product liability, plaintiffs can recover for design defects, manufacturing defects, and marketing (warning) defects. The 15-year statute of repose generally limits claims to products sold within the past 15 years, with exceptions.

Frequently asked

Questions people ask about defective product & product liability cases.

What if I don't have the product anymore?

Cases without the product are very difficult. If you still have it, preserve it exactly as it was at the time of injury.

Can I sue the retailer or only the manufacturer?

Texas law allows suits against non-manufacturing sellers in limited circumstances, typically when the manufacturer is insolvent or beyond jurisdiction.
