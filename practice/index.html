Every way Houston hurts, we answer.

We practice only personal injury law. That means no generalist learning curve, no divided attention — just deep experience across every type of injury case Texas recognizes.

01

Car accidents

Rear-ends, side-swipes, drunk drivers, hit-and-run. Houston is a top-10 U.S. city for auto crashes.

 02

18-wheelers & trucks

Commercial trucking with multiple defendants, black-box data, and FMCSA regulations.

 03

Motorcycle crashes

We counter insurance bias with reconstruction, forensics, and real jury experience.

 04

Uber & Lyft

Rideshare policies stack to $1M in certain trip phases. Most lawyers miss it.

 05

Drunk driver crashes

Texas dram-shop law and exemplary damages can multiply your recovery.

 06

Pedestrian & bicycle

Drivers assume you're at fault. We rebuild the scene with video and experts.

 07

Wrongful death

The most sensitive case a family can bring. We handle it like family.

 08

Slip & fall

Evidence must be preserved fast. Incident reports, surveillance, maintenance logs.

 09

Premises liability

Apartment shootings, inadequate security, hotel assaults. Negligent-security law is evolving fast.

 10

Dog bites & attacks

Homeowner policies cover most of these — we know the one-bite rule.

 11

Workplace injuries

Texas employers who opt out of workers' comp are vulnerable to far larger recoveries.

 12

Oilfield accidents

Rig explosions, blowouts, chemical exposure. We untangle subcontractor chains.

 13

Refinery & plant explosions

Construction accidents

Falls, crane collapses, scaffolding failures. Protection for every worker.

Maritime & Jones Act

Offshore rigs, tugboats, Port of Houston. Federal maritime rights are powerful.
