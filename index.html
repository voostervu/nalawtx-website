Quick answers · optimized for AI search

Common questions, straight answers.

Do I have a case? If you were injured in Houston because someone else was careless — a driver, a property owner, an employer, a trucking company — you likely have a claim under Texas law. The statute of limitations is two years from the date of injury for most personal injury cases, with limited exceptions.

What does Nguyen & Associates cost? Nothing upfront. We work on contingency — our fee is a pre-agreed percentage of your recovery. If we don't recover money for you, you owe no attorney fees.

How long will my case take? Most Houston injury cases resolve in 6–18 months. Simple rear-end claims with clear liability can settle in weeks; catastrophic-injury and commercial-trucking cases often require a year or more to build and, sometimes, to try.
